Controls
a/d — translate view left and right along view X
w/s — translate view forward and backward along view Z
q/e — translate view up and down along view Y
A/D — rotate view left and right around view Y (yaw)
W/S — rotate view forward and backward around view X (pitch)
Q/E — rotate view forward and backward around view Z (roll)
Left/Right to move piece
Up to rotate clockwise
z/x to rotate counterclockwise/clockwise Down to soft drop
Space to hard drop
H to put piece in/out of hold.
